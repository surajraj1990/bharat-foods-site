Frequently Asked Questions

What is cold-pressed oil?

Cold-pressed oil is extracted by mechanically pressing seeds or nuts without applying external heat. The process preserves all natural flavours, aromas, fatty acids, vitamins, and antioxidants — unlike refined oils processed with solvents and high heat.

How is Bharat Oils different from supermarket brands?

Most supermarket oils undergo solvent extraction, degumming, bleaching, and deodorisation — stripping natural nutrients and adding chemical residues. Bharat Oils uses only mechanical cold-pressing with zero chemical intervention from seed to bottle.

Are Bharat Oils safe for high-heat cooking?

Yes. Mustard Oil and Groundnut Oil have high smoke points suitable for deep frying and stir-frying. Sesame and Coconut Oil are best for medium-heat cooking. Each product page lists the recommended uses and smoke point.
